Peach Cobbler

Description

Here is a recipe for an easy to make peach cobbler. It does not require many ingredients and is quick to make. Enjoy with your favorite flavor of ice cream once complete.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pound of peaches
